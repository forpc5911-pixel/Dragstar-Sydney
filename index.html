What We Stand For

The Dragstar Ethos

01
Handmade in Sydney
Every garment cut, sewn and finished by hand in our Sydney studio. Never outsourced. Never rushed.
02
Ethical Always
Fair wages, local production, deadstock and vintage fabrics. Sustainability is our foundation, not our marketing.
03
Built to Last
We design for longevity, not trends. The same cut from five years ago still works with what we make today.
04
Your Wardrobe
Every new collection pairs with your existing Dragstar pieces. One wardrobe, forever growing.
